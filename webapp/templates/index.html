About the Project

The quality of welded joints is crucial in industries such as aerospace, defence and construction. Defects in welds can lead to catastrophic failures, making early detection essential. Traditional inspection methods such as manual inspection and radiographic analysis require skilled personnel and significant time. This project develops an automated inspection system using deep learning and Convolutional Neural Networks. A RetinaNet object detection model with a ResNet50 backbone is trained on welding defect datasets to detect defects such as cracks, porosity and inclusions. Users can upload welding images and the system automatically detects defects while visualizing their locations using bounding boxes.
